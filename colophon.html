I am Andreas Clemmensen. This is a personal log.

Colophon

This custom layout is hand-made by the author using HTML, CSS, and Jinja 2. Posts are drafted in Markdown, which are then processed using the author's fork of chisel to create and update this site. It is version-controlled by git, and served by github on the web.

Author

The author is an offshore structures and turret-mooring engineer. Outside of work, this site is his creative outlet.

Syndication and feedback

ckunte.net/log/rss feed is available for subscription via a news aggregator1. Feedback is welcome2, and may be sent to ten.etnukc@gol.
